COP3530 — Data Structures

Applied Data Structures: Blockchain, Digital Currency, and Beyond

This module bridges foundational data structures theory with real-world applications in blockchain technology and digital currency systems. Learners will explore how structures such as hash tables, trees, graphs, and linked lists underpin distributed ledgers, cryptographic verification, and decentralized finance — gaining both conceptual depth and practical insight into modern computing systems.
