COP3530 — Data Structures

Applied Data Structures: Blockchain, Digital Currency, and Beyond

This module bridges foundational data structures with real-world applications in blockchain technology and digital currency systems. Learners will explore how concepts such as hash tables, trees, graphs, linked lists, and cryptographic hashing underpin the architecture of modern decentralized systems, including Merkle trees, wallets, and the UTXO model.
