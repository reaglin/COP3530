Hash Tables: Structure and Collision Resolution

Explore the design and inner workings of hash tables — one of the most widely used data structures in software engineering. This module covers the fundamentals of hash table architecture, the properties of effective hash functions, and key collision resolution strategies including chaining and open addressing.
